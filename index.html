Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

  • Vulnerabilities reported to Naver Corporation will be disclosed to the public 45 days after releasing fixes to our customers. Extenuating circumstances, such as active exploitation, threats of an especially serious (or trivial) nature, or situations that require changes to an established standard may result in earlier or later disclosure. Disclosures made by Naver Corporation will include credit to the reporter unless otherwise requested by the reporter.


    Vulnerabilities will be disclosed in cve@navercorp.com.

  • Reporting Security Bugs

    All security bugs in Naver products are taken seriously and should be reported via bug bounty programs for each product or by emailing cve@navercorp.com. This will be delivered to Naver Security Team.
